Rockstar Band

Our Story

Rockstar band began in a small garage in Seattle, where five musicians with a shared dream of creating transformative music first came together. Founded in 2015, the band started as a passion project, blending rock, electronic, and indian influences.

Lead Yo Yo Honey Singh, Guru Randhawa and Jubin Nautiyal, Mika Singh, and Arijit Singh discovered their unique sound through countless late-night jam sessions and a relentless commitment to musical innovation.
